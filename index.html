Surg-R1

Hierarchical 3-level Reasoning for Surgical Scene Understanding

Surg-R1 is a surgical vision-language model with hierarchical chain-of-thought reasoning. It unifies perceptual grounding, relational understanding, and contextual reasoning across six surgical AI tasks and thirteen datasets.

Project Overview

Core Idea

We introduce a three-level reasoning hierarchy for surgical scene interpretation: perceptual grounding, relational understanding, and contextual reasoning.

Training Pipeline

A four-stage pipeline combines supervised fine-tuning, surgery-aware cold-start reasoning supervision, GRPO-based reinforcement learning, and iterative refinement.

Evaluation Scope

The model is evaluated on 13 datasets (7 public + 6 external) covering instrument localization, VQA, triplet recognition, phase/action recognition, and CVS assessment.
